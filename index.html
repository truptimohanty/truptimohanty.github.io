About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Prof. Taylor Sparks group, Materials Science & Engineering Department at the University of Utah, specializing in materials informatics and generative AI for crystal structure design. My research focuses on developing AI agent-based workflows for property-guided materials discovery, leveraging reinforcement learning and large language models to accelerate materials design.

Previously, I worked as a research scientist at the national laboratory (BARC, India) focusing on energy materials development and completed industry research projects at Apple, Inc.
